About AnimeVerse

AnimeVerse is a premium anime streaming platform built as a BCA final year project. Inspired by platforms like Netflix and Crunchyroll, we aimed to create a modern, responsive, and visually stunning anime streaming experience using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Our mission is to provide anime enthusiasts with a seamless platform to discover, track, and enjoy their favorite anime series and movies. With a dark futuristic theme and neon accents, AnimeVerse delivers an immersive viewing experience across all devices.
